This is a more skill-based version of rock paper scissors that takes to account the player's reaction time. Your objective is not to guess the opponent's pick, but rather to see it and react to it quickly. Once you press start, the opponent will slowly announce "jan, ken, pon!" and the player must react only after "pon!" is announced

There are three difficulty settings, each with a different reaction time requirement.

Good luck!
